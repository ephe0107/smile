    About this project

    Preventive oral health education for youth

    20 to 32 Smile Check is an educational project that helps young people connect everyday choices with oral health. The goal is to make prevention feel simple, encouraging, and interactive.

    🪥 Daily habits

    Brush, floss, and use fluoride toothpaste.

    💧 Smart choices

    Drink water and limit sugary snacks.

    😁 Prevention

    Checkups can catch problems early.
